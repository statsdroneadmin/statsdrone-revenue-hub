Building AI Agent Companies: The Future of Work with Andrew Ansley

📅 March 17, 2026 ⏱️ 46:26
▶ Play Episode View Details

About This Episode

In this episode, John Wright speaks with Andrew Ansley, an AI consultant focused on building agentic systems and autonomous AI workflows.

The conversation explores how AI agents are changing the way companies build products, run operations, and scale teams.

Andrew explains how the latest generation of AI tools allows developers and entrepreneurs to create systems that can analyze information, research problems, generate solutions, and execute tasks with minimal human oversight.

Rather than focusing purely on models, Andrew believes the real breakthrough is happening in agent harnesses — the systems that orchestrate AI tools, commands, and workflows.

Together they explore:

  • The shift from vibe coding to AI agents
  • How AI systems can automate complex workflows

  • Why smaller teams may outperform larger organizations

  • The growing importance of clear thinking and structured communication
  • What skills will matter most in the next decade

Andrew also shares practical advice for founders and operators looking to experiment with AI in their own businesses.

This episode is a deep dive into how AI agents may reshape work, startups, and software development over the next few years.

About This Episode

Sami Kurvinen is a long time friend in the affiliate marketing and iGaming industry. He was my favourite affiliate manager because he knew everything and he was hard working. Sami has evolved his career on all 3 pillars of the iGaming industry: first as a professional poker player, then an affiliate manager and now building his own affiliate sites. In this episode, Sami shares a lot of valuable insights that I think affiliate managers should pay attention to. Most affiliate managers focus on sales and are just doing their job, not really trying to evolve their career or to really understand the product they are promoting. I think this episode is valuable for anyone in iGaming or the affiliate marketing industry as a publisher or affiliate manager.

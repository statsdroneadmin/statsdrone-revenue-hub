# Designing for Agents with Jason Attard: UX, CX & The Future of Affiliate Sites

## Introduction

Welcome to another episode of the Affiliate BI Podcast. Today we're joined by Jason Attard to discuss the fascinating intersection of user experience, customer experience, and how AI agents are reshaping the future of affiliate websites.

In this episode, we dive deep into what it means to design digital experiences not just for humans, but for the AI agents that increasingly mediate our online interactions.

## The Evolution of UX in the Age of AI

Jason shares his insights on how traditional UX principles are being challenged and reimagined. "We used to design primarily for human eyeballs and human decision-making," he explains. "Now we need to consider how AI systems interpret, navigate, and recommend our content."

The conversation explores how affiliate marketers need to think beyond conventional SEO and consider the semantic structures that help AI agents understand and trust their content.

## Key Takeaways

- The importance of structured data and clear content hierarchies
- How AI agents evaluate trustworthiness differently than humans
- Practical strategies for optimizing affiliate sites for both human and AI audiences
- The future of conversational commerce and its impact on affiliate marketing

## Looking Ahead

As we wrap up, Jason offers his predictions for how the affiliate industry will evolve over the next few years, emphasizing the need for adaptability and continuous learning.

Podcast guesting vs hosting

📅 December 17, 2024 ⏱️ 6:18
▶ Play Episode View Details

About This Episode

Which is better? Ever since I've tuned in to the Confessions of a B2B Entrepreneur and heard Tom Hunt go head to head with Jakub Zajicek, I've thought hard about what is important. To be a guest on a podcast, or to host one?

I think the answer is a bit of both but guesting has a slight edge of building trust in a short period of time. Being a host means you're taking time to build your trust and persona.

Rev Share vs CPA

I know some affiliates that like their CPA payments but it is rare to find a casino affiliate that would walk away from their revenue share commissions.

I would suggest that people take rev share over CPA as your revenue share accounts themselves are actually part of a digital asset. It is recurring revenue where your CPA (cost per acquisition) will be a one and done payment.

If you're great at negotiating deals, you could consider a hybrid deal or revenue share and CPA.

Choosing the right affiliate software is one of the most important decisions an iGaming operator can make. Your affiliate program is only as good as the platform powering it. The software you choose affects everything from tracking accuracy and commission calculations to how affiliates perceive your program.

We work closely with several affiliate software providers and have had the opportunity to interview many of them on the Revenue Optimization podcast. These are platforms we know, trust and recommend to operators looking to run a professional affiliate program.

What Makes Great Affiliate Software?

The best affiliate software platforms share a few things in common. They provide accurate, real-time tracking that affiliates can trust. They offer flexible commission structures including revenue share, CPA and hybrid deals. They give operators the tools to manage large affiliate bases efficiently while giving affiliates the transparency they need to optimize their campaigns.

For iGaming specifically, you need a platform that understands the nuances of player lifecycle tracking, negative carryover calculations and multi-brand management. Not every affiliate software platform is built for iGaming, so choosing one with deep industry experience matters.

RavenTrack

RavenTrack is an affiliate tracking and management platform built specifically for the iGaming industry. They offer robust reporting, real-time data and a strong focus on transparency for both operators and affiliates. Their platform supports multi-brand management and has been gaining traction among operators who want a modern, reliable tracking solution.

iGsuite

iGsuite provides a comprehensive affiliate management platform with deep analytics, automated commission processing and a user-friendly interface. They are known for their strong customer support and willingness to customize the platform to meet specific operator needs. Read our full iGsuite review.

MyAffiliates

MyAffiliates has been in the affiliate software space for over a decade and is one of the most established platforms in iGaming. They power some of the largest affiliate programs in the industry and offer extensive customization, white-label solutions and a proven track record of handling high volumes of traffic and transactions.

ReferOn

ReferOn is a data-driven affiliate platform that focuses heavily on analytics and business intelligence. They help operators understand not just acquisition metrics but the full player journey, making it easier to identify which affiliates are driving the most valuable traffic. We had ReferOn on the podcast to discuss their approach to data-driven affiliate management.

Affilka

Affilka by SoftSwiss offers a modern affiliate management platform that integrates seamlessly with the SoftSwiss ecosystem. For operators already running on SoftSwiss, Affilka provides a native integration that simplifies setup and ensures accurate data flow between the casino platform and the affiliate system.

How We Evaluate Affiliate Software

Our reviews are based on direct experience, conversations with operators and affiliates who use these platforms, and interviews with the teams behind the software. We look at tracking accuracy, reporting depth, ease of use, affiliate experience, support quality and how well the platform scales as programs grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Affiliate software is a platform that allows operators and brands to manage their affiliate programs. It handles tracking, reporting, commission calculations, payment management and provides affiliates with marketing tools like tracking links and banners.

Consider your specific needs around tracking accuracy, reporting depth, payment automation, API integrations, multi-brand support and scalability. For iGaming operators, you will also want to ensure the platform supports dynamic variables, postbacks and player-level tracking.

Most modern affiliate platforms offer migration tools and API access to help transfer affiliate accounts, historical data and tracking configurations. The key is planning the migration carefully and working with both your current and new provider to ensure nothing is lost during the transition.

iGaming affiliates should prioritize platforms that offer real-time reporting, dynamic tracking variables, postback support, multi-currency handling, negative carryover transparency and detailed player lifetime value analytics. Integration with your existing tech stack is also important.
